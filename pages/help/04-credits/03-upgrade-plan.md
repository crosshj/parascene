## Why upgrade

Everyone starts on the **Free** plan. You can:

- Create with AI and generative tools.
- Claim free credits daily (when available).
- Share creations, build a profile, and explore the community.

If you create often, upgrading gives you **more credits and reliability** so you can focus on making things instead of
watching your balance.

## Plans and credits

Current plans are shown on the [pricing page](/pricing). At a high level:

- **Free**
  - $0 — always.
  - A smaller pool of credits.
  - Daily credit claims when available.
  - Full access to core features (create, share, comment, follow, explore).

- **Founder** (paid subscription)
  - More credits (currently **700 credits per month**).
  - Priority support.
  - Early feature consideration.
  - **Founder flair** on your profile (lifetime).

Exact amounts, prices, and perks may change over time — the [pricing page](/pricing) is the source of truth for the
current offer.

## How to upgrade

You can upgrade at any time:

- **From the Credits modal**
  - Click your credit balance in the header to open the **Credits** modal.
  - In the **Get more credits** section, choose **View plans** to open the pricing page.

- **From the pricing page**
  - Go to the [pricing page](/pricing) from the main site or footer.
  - Choose the plan you want and follow the prompts to subscribe or switch.

After checkout:

- Your plan is updated as soon as payment is confirmed.
- Your available credits and perks adjust automatically.
- You can switch back to Free from the pricing page later if you want.

## How upgrading affects tipping

For safety and abuse prevention, brand‑new accounts may have a short waiting period before they can tip. Upgrading your
plan lifts this limit:

- **Free, new accounts** – may see a temporary “you can’t tip yet” message.
- **Upgraded accounts** – can usually tip right away.

For more about tipping, see [Tipping](/help/credits/tipping).
