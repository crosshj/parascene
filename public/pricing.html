Start creating for free. Credits power creation and participation—earn them by being part of the community.

How it works

parascene uses a credits system for creation and certain actions. You don’t need a credit card to get started: you earn credits by participating (e.g., daily claims, engagement), and you spend them to create, boost servers, or join competitions where supported. Value stays in the community.

No hidden fees

We don’t sell subscriptions for basic creation. You can participate fully by earning and spending credits. Optional paid top-ups or tipping may be offered in-app where available; those will be clearly labeled and subject to the payment terms shown at the time of purchase.
